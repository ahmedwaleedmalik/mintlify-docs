## Get Up and Running in Three Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your repo">
    Link your private git repository to Google Docs. We support GitHub, GitLab, and Bitbucket — no extra credentials or infrastructure required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure navigation">
    Add a `docs.json` file to the root of your repo to define your site's structure, navigation, and branding in one place.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Push your changes to git. Google Docs detects the update and automatically builds and publishes your site — your docs are live instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
